Support us so we can continue working!

Besides this project doesn't pursue a financial goal, we do have various spences:

  1. Web site building and hosting
  2. Genealogical research expenses: related to Internet, transport and archives fees (national archives, parish records, civil register)
  3. Buying DNA kits for our selected lines

If you wish, you can consider donating and that way we can do more tests and improve the genealogy results. Also, it can help us burst the development of the website (all is being made from scratch). See the button to donate through PayPal below.

You can also support us by using our genealogy service and by subscribing to our Patreon, where we share our research notes.
