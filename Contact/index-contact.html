How to follow and stay in touch with the project?

The project will have an online site, which is under construction. Additionally, the project will keep all followers and users informed through social networks: Facebook (@magenha, #magenha) and Reddit (u/magenha), as well as on the website under construction. Any doubt, question, suggestion, offer to collaborate in any aspect, can be communicated through the email contactmagenha@gmail.com.
