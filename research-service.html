Forms

The following links lead to forms intended to collect the applicant's data, and those required to locate the document.

Select the type of document you wish to request, and complete the information on the form. Remember that these are only parish documents in Cuba.

If you provide accurate data, this will ensure the success of the search, and shorten the delivery time. We recommend to consult our list of burial records in our Patreon. Also, consult our suggestions of documentary sources in Cuba.

Disclaimer: We can only work in the parishes indicated on the form. If you want to know more, you can contact us.

Terms and conditions of the service

In order to obtain funds to carry out our investigations, and to buy DNA kits, MAGENHA offers a genealogical search service for documents in Cuba.

The interested person must provide data in the forms at the end.

Below are the terms and conditions of use of the service.

PAYMENTS AND REFUNDS

  1. Once we have results on the research, we will reach you and schedule the payment.
  2. We will not provide research results until payment is made in full.
  3. It will only be charged if the investigation has results and not otherwise.
  4. The amount will be told in advance in the contact email, but it will be between 37-50% of the cost with genealogist services in Cuba.
  5. Payments will be received using our donation button via PayPal.

Research and completion time

Given that these investigations will be included in our investigations as a project, it is not possible to specify a time for the completion of each order. It depends on the place where the document should be consulted and the volume of searches of our fundamental lines of investigation.

Sharing information

The person receives a transcript of the document, or a parish certificate

Each document delivered by MAGENHA will contain a watermark, since when they are searched by the project, they become part of our documentary collection.

Reproductions should constitute (1) select quotations and/or readers’ original summaries with (2) a link to the post rather than extensive reproductions of the post’s contents (i.e., more than two paragraphs).
