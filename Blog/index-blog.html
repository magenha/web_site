Learn about haplogroups

Explore haplogroups and their significance in understanding our ancient origins and migration patterns through DNA analysis. Learn about the different haplogroups associated with Native Americans, Guanche people of the Canary Islands, West Africans, and Iberians. Discover how analysis of ancient DNA has provided insights into our ancestors, such as Ötzi the Iceman. This classification system sheds light on our shared history and connections between diverse populations worldwide.

Tras las huellas africanas

A través de un emocionante examen de ADN, exploramos la historia genética de una persona cuyos antepasados se arraigaron en San José de las Lajas, Cuba. Su haplogrupo paterno E-M2 revela vínculos ancestrales africanos en los pueblos de Tapaste y Santa María del Rosario
