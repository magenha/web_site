Our goal:

Our research project aims to uncover and document the genetic ancestry of diverse populations in Latin America, Spain, Portugal and Africa. By conducting extensive genealogical research and DNA testing, we seek to provide valuable insights into the historical migration patterns, origin stories, and genetic diversity of various communities. Our mission is to make this knowledge accessible and free to all, especially those with limited resources. Through collaboration with volunteers and donors, we strive to advance our research, expand our database, and promote a deeper understanding of human genetic heritage.

To support our mission, we kindly encourage individuals to consider making a donation towards our research project.

ABOUT US

MAGENHA is a project initiated by Samuel Martínez Alcalá in 2022

Motivated by our deep passion for genealogy and genetics, we embarked on this project with the belief that these disciplines hold the key to captivating discoveries, shaping the genetic identity of Cuba, the Canary Islands, pre-Columbian America, and Africa.
